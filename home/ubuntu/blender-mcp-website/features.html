Scene Setup & Asset Importing

Create complete environments and import assets with simple text prompts.

Mixamo Integration

Seamlessly import and rig characters from Mixamo's extensive library. Choose from hundreds of pre-rigged characters and automatically import them into your Blender scene.

Marketplace Integrations

Direct importing from Sketchfab, TurboSquid, and Quixel Megascans. Search and import high-quality 3D models, textures, and assets without leaving Blender.

HDRI Lighting Automation

Apply lighting automatically based on scene descriptions. Choose from a library of HDRI environments or describe the lighting you want, and let the AI set it up for you.

Procedural Environment Generation

Generate terrain, architecture, and complete environments from text descriptions. Create mountains, forests, cities, and more with simple prompts.

Character Animation & Motion Control

Create dynamic character animations and cinematic camera movements with AI assistance.

Rigify Auto-Rigging

Automatically rig custom characters with Blender's Rigify system. Import any character model and let the AI create a complete rig with proper weight painting.

Animation Retargeting

Transfer animations between different rigs with intelligent bone mapping. Use animations from Mixamo or other sources on your custom characters.

IK/FK Switching

Seamlessly switch between Inverse Kinematics and Forward Kinematics for animation flexibility. Perfect for creating complex character movements.

AI-Driven Keyframe Generation

Generate animations from text descriptions. Describe the movement you want, and let the AI create the keyframes for you.

Cinematic Camera Work

Create dynamic camera movements, tracking shots, and multi-camera setups. Set up depth of field, focus pulls, and other cinematic camera techniques with ease.
